Hi, I'm **Mateusz Fejcher**, a passionate embedded systems engineer with the ambition to get better every day.

I build applications and entire systems with **Qt**, and craft custom embedded
Linux distributions with **Yocto** for real-world, real-time embedded
projects - from bring-up on target hardware to the UI running on top of it.

I'm a **certified Qt developer**, mostly working in **QML** with some
experience in **Widgets**, and I also have some experience with **LVGL**.

## What this blog is

This is my journal of the projects I take on - some small, some bigger.
Each post documents something I built: the problem I was solving, the
approach I took, what went wrong along the way, and the result. No polish,
just the actual work - Qt widgets and QML, Yocto layers and recipes,
cross-compilation headaches, board bring-up, and everything in between.

More than a portfolio, it's a record of the journey - proof that I deliver,
and a way to track how I'm growing as an engineer, one project at a time.
